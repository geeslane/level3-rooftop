Dress Code

Level 3 is a space for those who take their evenings seriously. We uphold a smart elegant dress code that honours the calibre of the venue and the company you keep.

Smart Elegant Required — cocktail dresses, tailored suits, elegant separates.
Sports Attire Not Permitted — no tracksuits, jerseys, or athletic wear.
Footwear — no open-toed sandals for gentlemen. Heels and dress shoes preferred.
Caps & Hats — not permitted on the club floor after midnight.
Management reserves the right to refuse entry at their discretion.
