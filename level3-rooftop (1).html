About Level 3

The Vision

A New Standard
of Luxury

Level 3 Rooftop was conceived from a singular vision: to create Port Harcourt's most sophisticated and exclusive destination where culinary artistry, world-class entertainment, and breathtaking skyline views converge into one extraordinary experience.

We believe that true luxury is not merely opulence — it is the seamless orchestration of atmosphere, service, and experience. Every element of Level 3 has been crafted with intentional precision, from the curated menu of our executive chef to the architecture of sound in our club.

Entry to Level 3 is not simply a ticket — it is an invitation into an inner circle. We maintain an intimate capacity to ensure that every guest receives the immersive, personal experience they deserve.

Exclusive Nightlife

The Club

The Inner Circle

Nightlife at its
Most Refined

Level 3's club is not for everyone — by design. We maintain strict capacity limits, ensuring that those inside experience something genuinely rare: an exclusive space where energy, luxury, and discretion coexist.

Our sound system was engineered for the rooftop environment. Our resident DJs are among the best in Nigeria. And our bar programme features handcrafted cocktails built by world-class mixologists.

Entry to the club is by approval only. Guest list, bottle service, and VIP tables can be arranged through our team.

VIP Table Packages

Secure your space above the crowd. All packages include dedicated service, premium bottle selections, and priority entry.

Onyx
Entry-level VIP table for groups of up to 4. Includes 1 premium bottle and reserved seating.
Gold
Premium VIP table for up to 8 guests. Includes 2 bottles, dedicated host, and rooftop terrace access.
Platinum
Our most exclusive tier. Full private booth, bespoke cocktail experience, and direct artist meet.
