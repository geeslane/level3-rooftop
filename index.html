About Level 3

Perched above the vibrant heart of Port Harcourt, Level 3 Rooftop is a sanctuary of sophistication. We are more than a venue — we are an experience crafted for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

From the moment you ascend to our rooftop, the city unfolds beneath you while our world of culinary excellence, live performances, and nightlife royalty comes alive. Every evening is an occasion. Every night is a memory.
